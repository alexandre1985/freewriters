About

This project is a vision of Daniel Santos to enrich the world’s culture by giving oportunity to every person to produce his/her’s own book. It maybe be a common or a unique story, but is one that you produced in form of a book.

The tools have been here for a while (I’m talking about the TeX technology) and it is time that they be known to the general public.

You can make a book, we can teach you. And the best is that it is cheap ... all you need is a computer and a brain.
