Empowering Writers

We want to make it possible for every individual to write one or more books

IS OUR WHISH TO

MAKE IT EASY

Writers should only be focused on creating the content. Let the rest with us

MAKE IT AFFORDABLE

Every person of society, independent of his or hers bank account, should be able to create a book without being limited

MAKE THE RIGHTS YOURS

We want you, not the publisher, to have the rights to your finished works, so that you can decide what you wish to do with it

EVERYONE HAS A GOOD STORY TO TELL

We believe your stories will make a difference. Maybe in someone’s life or in an entire group. We will give you the resources you need to acomplish your work.

Your books are the seed. Plant it and leave the world a better place … an even more beautiful one.
